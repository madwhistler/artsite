Themes

I have a number of themes I am exploring in my artwork. 'Lavanic' art is in its own quadrant, as is "BEAR". Other themes you may find here:

  • Flora and Fauna - much of my art is derived from nature, and you'll find a number of studies that relate to the natural settings around my home in North Georgia. I'm particularly fond of Muscadines!
  • Intuitive - I have a number of pieces that are more intuitive than representational. These are often done in gouache or watercolor.
  • Cat - A series featuring "Cat without a stomach" and related acrylics.
  • Ironic - Lavanic Irony is a theme that I am exploring. It is the idea that beauty is often found in the unexpected.
  • Objective - I have a number of pieces that are more objective.
  • Form - A number of form studies.

You'll also find some galleries for specific media, such as oils, textiles, and so on.

Explore, enjoy, and flag your favorites which will show up under "YOU"
